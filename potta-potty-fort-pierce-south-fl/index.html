Frequently Asked Questions

How many portable restrooms do I need for my event?

Our team recommends one unit per 50 guests for a four-hour event. Larger or longer events may require additional units, and we help you calculate the right number.

What happens if a porta potty needs servicing during my rental?

Our maintenance crew is on call for cleanings, restocking, and repairs. Simply contact our office, and we’ll respond quickly.

What rental options are available?

We provide standard, deluxe with sinks, ADA-compliant, and emergency porta-potty rentals.

Can I rent for a long-term project?

Yes, porta-potties can be rented for weeks or months, with flexible maintenance schedules.

Are your units certified for compliance?

All porta-potties meet OSHA, ADA, and health code requirements, backed by our professional certifications.

Do you offer restroom trailers for upscale events?

Yes, we provide luxury restroom trailers with sinks, lighting, and flushing toilets.
